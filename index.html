What is the pool calculator?
The pool calculator is a tool that calculate the volume of water you'll need to fill a pool which will help you estemate the cost of the water, as well as the amount of time you need to fill the pool.
How to calculate pool volume?
To calculate pool volume you should use the following formula:
v = l * w * (d1 + d2) / 2
Where:
v = Pool volume
l = Length of the pool
w = Width of the pool
d1 = Depth at one end of the pool
d2 = Depth at another end of the pool
